### Highlights

- Retro Studio with 24 curated presets spanning the 1950s through the 1990s.
- Style Library search with decade, color, and mood filters.
- 4K exports, caption builder, and basic integrations with Canva and Dropbox.

### Fixes and improvements

- Stabilized upload pipeline for large PNG files.
- Improved skin tone rendering across varied lighting conditions.
- Added audit logs for enterprise workspaces.
