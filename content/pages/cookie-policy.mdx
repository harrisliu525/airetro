## Overview

AI Retro uses cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to remember your preferences and improve product performance. By using the site you consent to the use of cookies described in this policy.

## Types of cookies we use

- **Essential cookies:** Required for authentication, security, and credit usage tracking.
- **Functional cookies:** Remember language, theme mode, and most recently used presets.
- **Analytics cookies:** Help us understand product adoption and spot usability issues. We anonymize analytics data and never sell it.

## Managing cookies

Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Doing so may limit access to certain AI Retro features such as saved presets or integrations.

## Updates

We review this policy regularly as we release new functionality. Significant changes will be communicated via in-app notifications or email.
