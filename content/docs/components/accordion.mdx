## When to use

Use the accordion to group FAQs, preset notes, or workflow steps without overwhelming the page.

## Content guidelines

Keep titles under 48 characters and pair them with concise descriptions. The component supports markdown so you can add links to sample galleries.

## Customization

Update iconography, border radius, and default open states in src/components/docs/accordion.tsx to match your brand.
