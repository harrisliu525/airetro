## Use cases

Promote new presets, trial extensions, or seasonal campaigns. Place the banner near the top of landing pages or dashboards.

## Styling options

Configure background gradients, icon choices, and CTA labels. The banner supports dark and light modes automatically.

## Best practices

Keep copy under 80 characters and include a single clear call to action.
