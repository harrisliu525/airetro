## Adding zoomable images

Wrap an ImageZoom component around any screenshot or render. Provide alt text that describes the subject and era.

## Zoom experience

Users click to open a modal with keyboard and touch support. Captions appear below the image to provide context.

## Performance notes

Compress assets and serve AVIF or WebP where supported to keep pages fast.
