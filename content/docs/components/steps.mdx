## Structure

Each step includes a title, short description, and optional icon. Keep descriptions under 90 characters for readability.

## Styling

Choose between horizontal or vertical layouts depending on available space. Progress indicators can be enabled for multi-phase tutorials.

## Content tips

Use action-oriented verbs like "Upload", "Select", and "Share" to keep the flow energetic.
