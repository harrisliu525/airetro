## Variants

Use info, success, warning, and danger variants to signal the tone of your message.

## Content guidelines

Keep callouts under three sentences. Provide links to related resources when actionable steps are required.

## Customization

Adjust icons and border colors to align with your brand palette stored in the docs theme.
