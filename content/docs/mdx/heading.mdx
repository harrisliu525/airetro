## Hierarchy

Follow the H1-H6 structure shown in this guide. Use sentence case and action-oriented verbs where possible.

## Short vs long headings

Keep H2 headings under 60 characters. Break longer ideas into multiple headings for readability.

## Accessibility

Headings should describe the content that follows so screen reader users can navigate easily.
