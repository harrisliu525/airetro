## Keyword search

Enter moods, eras, props, or lighting terms. Results show preview cards with sample renders and recommended use cases.

## Filters

Filter by decade, orientation, medium, and color profile. Combine filters to narrow down to specific scenarios like "1980s portrait + neon + portrait orientation."

## Collections

Bookmark presets into collections for campaigns. Collections sync across the workspace and can be shared externally as mood boards.

## Recent activity

The search page lists recent presets used by your team so you can recreate successful looks quickly.
