## Export formats

Choose PNG, JPG, GIF loop, or MP4 video loop. Creator and Studio plans include PSD exports with transparent backgrounds.

## Aspect ratios

Select from square, portrait (4:5), landscape (16:9), or poster (2:3). AI Retro crops intelligently and preview grids show safe zones.

## Metadata

Enable metadata export to include preset name, prompt, caption, and usage license in a JSON sidecar.

## Automation

Use the integration settings to send exports directly to your storage bucket, design tool, or storefront.
