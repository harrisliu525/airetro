## Hero composition

Pair a bold headline with social proof and a Retro Studio preview. Include a primary CTA and optional secondary action.

## Responsive behavior

Use stacked layouts on mobile and preserve aspect ratio for preview media. Test with light and dark themes.

## Content guidance

Highlight the core benefit in the first sentence and include key differentiators such as era accuracy and 4K exports.
