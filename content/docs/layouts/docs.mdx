## Layout regions

The docs layout includes a header, sidebar navigation, content column, and footer metadata. Toggle sections on or off based on your information architecture.

## Customization

Override the theme tokens for spacing, typography, and background colors to align with your brand.

## Tips

Keep the main content width under 860px for optimal readability and ensure the sidebar supports keyboard navigation.
