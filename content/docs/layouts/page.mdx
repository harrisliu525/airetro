## Structure

Header with breadcrumbs, content column, and optional right rail for quick links or signup forms.

## Custom blocks

Insert testimonials, call-to-action banners, or pricing snippets using MDX slots.

## Accessibility

Ensure heading levels are sequential and use color contrast that passes WCAG 2.1 AA.
